Guzheng

Guzheng is a traditional instrument of Chinese Han nationality. It is one of the unique and important national musical instruments in China. It has beautiful timbre, wide range, rich playing skills and strong expressiveness, so it is deeply loved by the masses. I have been studying guzheng since I was four years old, and now it has been sixteen years. Learning an instrument helps to improve our patience.
